Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to GYMTRACK ME. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you visit our website and use our services.

By using GYMTRACK ME, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please refrain from using our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to enhance our services and provide a better user experience:

  • Personal Information
    • Name, email address, phone number.
    • Payment details (for premium features and subscription services).
  • Fitness & Health Data
    • Workout history, exercise logs, progress tracking, and related inputs.
    • User-provided health and fitness goals.
  • Device & Usage Data
    • IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers.
    • Log files, access timestamps, and referral information.
  • Cookies & Tracking Technologies
    • We use cookies and tracking technologies to enhance the user experience, analyze website performance, and provide personalized content.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used in the following ways:

  • To provide, improve, and personalize our fitness tracking services.
  • To offer customized workout recommendations.
  • To send important updates, newsletters, and promotional offers.
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions.
  • To enhance security and prevent fraudulent activities.

4. Cookies Policy

a. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us improve your experience by remembering preferences, analyzing website usage, and delivering personalized content.

b. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for core website functions such as login authentication and session management.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect analytics on website usage to help us improve functionality.
  • Functional Cookies: Store user preferences to enhance website experience.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used for personalized advertising and promotional offers.

c. Managing Cookies

  • Users can accept or decline cookies when visiting our website.
  • Cookie settings can be adjusted via browser preferences.
  • Declining cookies may impact certain website features and functionalities.

5. Data Sharing & Security

a. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell or rent personal data to third parties. However, we may share your data with:
  • Service Providers: Payment processors, analytics tools, and customer support platforms.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law or to protect our legal rights.

b. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures such as encryption, secure storage, and access controls to protect your information.

6. User Rights

  • Access & Control: Users can access, update, or delete their personal data at any time.
  • Marketing Preferences: Users can opt out of marketing emails through their account settings.
  • Cookies & Tracking: Users can adjust their browser settings to manage cookies.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any significant changes will be communicated to users via email or website notifications.

8. Contact Information

For any privacy-related concerns, contact support@gymtrackme.com.
