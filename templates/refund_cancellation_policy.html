Refund & Cancellation Policy

1. No Refund Policy

All payments made for GYMTRACK subscriptions are non-refundable.

Refunds will not be issued for subscription cancellations, unused periods, or dissatisfaction with the service.

2. Free Trial & Subscription Plans

New users receive a one-month free trial before being required to select a paid plan.

Available plans include Monthly, 3-Month, 6-Month, and Yearly subscriptions.

Once a subscription is activated, no refunds will be provided.

3. Cancellation Policy

Users can cancel their subscription anytime through their account settings.

Cancellation will be effective at the end of the current billing cycle.

Users will retain access until the subscription period ends but will not be charged for the next cycle.

4. Contact for Refund & Billing Queries

For billing inquiries or subscription management, contact billing@gymtrackme.com.
